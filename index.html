about TV GIrl

TV Girl is an American Indie pop band

from San Diego, consisting of Brad

Petering, Jason Wyman, and Wyatt Harmon.

The group is now based in Los Angeles.

They have 5 studio albums out:

French Exit (2014), Who Really Cares (2016),

Death of a Party Girl (2018), The Night

in Question: French Exit Outtakes (2020),

and Aestheticadelica (2020).
